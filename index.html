No Frills Focus

A focus tool for macOS.

It does one thing

You pick the app you want to work in.
No Frills Focus covers everything else on your screen to prevent distractions.

What this is

No Frills Focus enforces a commitment you’ve already made to sit down and work.

It doesn’t organize your day.
It doesn’t teach anything.
It doesn’t care what you’re working on.

It keeps you honest.

We don’t track.
We don't do push notifications.
We don’t hold your work.
You use the tools and apps you already trust.

How it works

Pick the app or document you want to work in.
Choose a session time from the menu. Or just work until you’re done.

Getting out

Quitting mid-session is always an option.

We just make not working a conscious decision
  • Normal exit: Cmd + Shift + X
  • Emergency quit: Cmd + Shift + Z
Design philosophy

Most focus apps try to motivate.
Ours assumes you already know what deserves your attention.
We keep it from drifting.

Privacy

No Frills Focus doesn’t see your files or touch your apps.
