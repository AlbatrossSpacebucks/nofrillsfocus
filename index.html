No Frills Focus

A focus tool for the macOS; designed to be the most boring app you will ever own.

What it is

No Frills Focus hides everything on your desktop except what you're working on, allowing for a deep, distraction-free work session.

Use the writing tools and apps you already trust.

How it works

Open the app or document you want to work in.
Choose a 15, 30, or 60 min session.
Or just work until you’re done.

Getting out

Work or quit, we're not the boss of you. We just make quitting a conscious decision instead of a passive one.

  • Normal exit: Cmd + Shift + X
  • Emergency quit: Cmd + Shift + Z
Design philosophy

Our desktop masking is fixed by design.
Constraint is the point.
The less you see, the less you drift.

Privacy

No Frills Focus doesn’t host your work, trade your data, or care how boring you think we are.
